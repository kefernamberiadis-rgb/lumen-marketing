Frequently asked questions

What is Lumen Flow?

Lumen Flow is a wellness app that gently follows the rhythm of your menstrual cycle and the phases of the moon. It includes a cycle calendar, a fasting timer, daily check-ins, nourishment guidance, a private journal, and short learning content — all designed to support gentle daily awareness rather than strict tracking or scoring.

Does Lumen Flow replace medical advice?

No. Lumen Flow is a wellness and reflection tool, not a medical device, and it does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Always speak with a qualified healthcare professional about any medical, hormonal, fertility, or health concerns.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team any time at lumenfuxbiz@gmail.com. We aim to respond to all messages as quickly as we can.

How do I request data deletion?

Email us at lumenfuxbiz@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will confirm receipt and remove your data from our systems in line with our Privacy Policy.

How do subscriptions work?

If Lumen Flow offers a subscription, it is billed and managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on where you downloaded the app. You can view, change, or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's account or subscription settings.
