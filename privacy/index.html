Privacy Policy

How Lumen Flow handles your information.

Last updated · 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumen Flow ("the app," "we," "us") handles information when you use the app. We've written it in plain language so it's easy to understand. If anything is unclear, you're welcome to contact us at the email address below.

Information the app may include

Depending on how you use Lumen Flow, the app may include the following types of information that you choose to enter:

  • Cycle tracking information, such as period dates and cycle length
  • Fasting activity, including fasting windows and timing
  • Daily check-ins, such as mood, energy, sleep, and body notes
  • Journal entries and personal reflections
  • Symptoms you choose to log
  • Preferences and settings within the app

You are always in control of what you choose to enter. None of this information is required to use the basic features of the app.

How this information is used

Any information you enter is used to provide the core features of Lumen Flow to you — for example, displaying your cycle calendar, calculating fasting windows, showing your check-in history, and surfacing your journal entries. We do not use this information to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Payments and subscriptions

If Lumen Flow offers any paid features or subscriptions, all payment processing is handled directly by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play), depending on your device. Lumen Flow does not directly collect or store your payment card details. Please refer to Apple's or Google's own privacy policies for information about how they handle payment data.

Data storage

Information you enter into Lumen Flow is stored in order to provide the app's features to you, such as showing your history and trends over time. We take reasonable steps to protect this information from unauthorized access.

Your choices and rights

You can contact us at any time to ask questions about the information associated with your use of the app, or to request that it be deleted. To request data deletion, email us at the address below with the subject line "Data Deletion Request," and we will confirm and process your request.

Children's privacy

Lumen Flow is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
